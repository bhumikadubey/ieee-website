About IEEE-TCET.

IEEE TCET Students Forum provides a platform for all students to enhance their technical skills and expand their knowledge in all domain. The members of IEEE benifit from the seminars,workshops and activities that are planned internationally renowned Committee and have access to IEEE's website. The members also get access to the monthly Technical Magazine by IEEE which keeps them updated with the latest trends around the world The members are also allowed to participate in International Conferences and Symposium's organized by IEEE all around the world. Currently IEEE-TCET has 100 members associated with it IEEE TCET Mumbai is a student chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). We are a student-run organization at the TCET, our motives are to find ourselves and our passions, to have fun and meet new people, gather new experiences, to learn industrial skills to enhance ourselves, to learn about research and innovation in technology, to help our community and to get inspired to change the world!

Check our Events

Machine Learning Bootcamp

The bootcamp is a specialized program aiming to equip students with machine learning skills for geospatial data analysis; covering fundamental concepts and practical applications, participants will learn to apply ML algorithms to geological, environmental, and remote sensing prestored datasets.Thereby harnessing machine learning's potential.

Flip N Chug

The Bottle Flip game invites players to skillfully flip partially filled water bottles, aimimg for upright landings. Successful flippers enjoy a refreshmg dnnk as a reward. Laughter and cheers fill the air as the player that empties their bottles first emerges victorious in this lively and entertatning challenge

Balloon Olympics

Balloon Olympics is a playful event where participants engage in various balloon-based challenges. Events include balloon races, where indivisuals race crossing the obstacles while keeping balloons afloat. It's a lighthearted and energetic affair, fostering laughter as participants showcase their balloon-handling skills in a festive, competitive spirit

Car Simulation

In a Car Simulation activity enriched with VR technology, participants dive jnto a lifelike driving experience using advanced simulators. VR enhances the sensation, they navigate virtual roads, mastering steering, braking, and parking with the provided physical steering wheel.

Lazer Tag

Laser tag is a team-based shooting game where players use infrared-emitting light guns to tag opponents, with the goal of achieving the highest score and winning the game.

Customisable Resin workshop

This dynamic workshop empowers participants to craft their personalized keychains and coasters, utilizing resin as the primary medium. All participants receive a comprehensive kit that includes high-quality resin, essential tools, and an array of decorative materials, such as glitter, dried flowers, and pigments.
